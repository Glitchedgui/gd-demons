How 2 detect demons in gd

Created by Thelostboss

Easy demon

To know an easy demon, some stuff is that it is purple and has blue eyes 2 teeth and 2 small horns.

Medium demon

A medium demon is a pinkish color, it has blue eyes like the easy demon has 2 horns again like the easy demon but has 4 teeth.

Hard demon

The hard demon is red, has 2 long horns, has green eyes, and it's teeth is clenching.

Insane demon

The insane demon is red again, it has 2 long horns and 2 short horns, has firey eyes (sort of), and this time its teeth isn't clenching.

Extreme demon

The demon is a darker type of red, has 2 long horns, 2 short, 2 small, has firey eyes (again) and its teeth isn't clenching.
